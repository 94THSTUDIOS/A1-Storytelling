"Visualizing the Impact of Mental Health: A Global and Local Perspective"

Mental health profoundly influences every aspect of human life, from individual well-being to societal productivity. The World Health Organization (WHO) emphasizes that mental health conditions are a leading cause of disability worldwide, while the Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH) underscores their widespread impact in Canada. Beyond individual challenges, untreated mental health issues contribute to significant economic burdens, including lost productivity and increased healthcare costs. This data visualization project explores the complex impacts of mental health, aiming to highlight the importance of awareness, access to care, and effective interventions.

Who Is Affected?

"Mental health challenges disproportionately affect young people aged 15-24."
